This mod adds Fargo's Souls compatibility with multiple mods on the Steam workshop, such as:
Spirit,
Vitality,
Orchid,
Dragon Ball,
Bombus Apis,
and Polarities.

This mod also has heavy crossmodding with Community Souls Expansion and Secrets of The Souls.
It is highly recommended to play with those two mods enabled if you have the mods they support.

Source code: https://github.com/Ropro0923/SoulsofResonance
